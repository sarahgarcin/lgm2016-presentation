## Why ? We had a problem… When someone shares a piece of knowledge, note takers transcript, in their own way, the same content. But, we can't pull our efforts together and mutualize, in real-time, our notes.
## …which had not been solved… Collaborative text editors like Etherpad or Googledocs already exist. [Tools for collaborative writing](http://c.sarahgarcin.com/champ)
## So we cracked our own stuff… Our tool’s main goal is to allow a personal note taking and, in the same time, a visualisation of the other contributors’ notes. This mutualisation produces an online document upgraded by everyone’s work.

## Which practices? Which applications? - Brainstorming - Meetings - Courses - Conferences - Festivals More broadly, people sharing knowledge at the same time, in the same place.
